How to Play

a step-by-step guide to your game

PICK Daily Games offer players more ways to win!

So ... how do you play it?

Use the Florida Lottery app to create a digital play slip or fill one out at a Florida Lottery retailer. You can also purchase directly from a Florida Lottery vending machine. Complete a PICK 2, 3, 4, or 5 play slip. Choose your favorite numbers 0-9 on each column, or select Quick Pick to let the terminal pick some or all of your numbers at random. Then select your play type. Be sure to select the amount, draw time and the number of consecutive days for your numbers to be played!

Add FIREBALL by checking the FIREBALL box on the left side of the playslip. FIREBALL will double the cost of your PICK ticket and apply to all panels played.

FIREBALL is an additional drawing that takes place directly following the twice-daily PICK game drawings.

Only one FIREBALL number will be drawn, applying to all four PICK Daily Games.

If you've chosen to add FIREBALL, you can REPLACE one of the OFFICIAL PICK winning numbers drawn with the FIREBALL number drawn to CREATE more combinations.

You can even WIN TWICE ... with both the base game and FIREBALL!

Playing Pick 3

  1. Get your play slip

    Visit any Florida Lottery retail location for a paper play slip to choose your numbers. Every PICK 3 play slip contains 5 panels. Tickets for each drawing may be purchased up to 11 minutes prior to the applicable drawing – 1:19 p.m., ET for the midday drawing and 9:34 p.m., ET for the evening drawing.

    Find a retailer

  2. Mark your panels

    Choose a panel and select one number (from 0 through 9) from each column for a total of three digits. You may choose the same number in multiple columns or switch it up. Or, mark the QP (Quick Pick) box and let the terminal randomly select some or all of your numbers. For a Front Pair play, select a number from the first two columns only; for a Back Pair play, select a number from the last two columns only.

    Select your play type: 

    • Straight: Match numbers in EXACT ORDER to win. 
    • Box: Match numbers in ANY ORDER to win. 
    • Straight/Box: Match in EXACT or ANY order to win.
    • Combo: Play all EXACT ORDER combinations of your numbers for the chance to win.
    • Front Pair:  Match the first two numbers to win.
    • Back Pair:  Match the last two numbers to win. 

    Select the amount you want to play: $0.50 or $1 (all Straight/Box plays cost $1; Combo plays cost $1.50 to $6.00).

  3. Choose your add-on's

    Add FIREBALL to replace one of the official PICK winning numbers drawn with the FIREBALL number drawn to create more winning combinations. Adding FIREBALL doubles your ticket price and applies to all panels played.

    Save time with Advance Play

    To play the same numbers for more than one draw, select the number of advance draws you wish to play in the Advance Play section at the top of your play slip. If you claim a prize on a winning Advance Play ticket before all of the draws on the ticket have occurred, you will be issued a continuation ticket for the remaining draws with the same play numbers as the original ticket.

  4. Select your draw time

    PICK 3 drawings are held twice a day. Choose MID for the midday drawing at 1:30 p.m. ET, EVE for the evening drawing  at 9:45 p.m., ET, or BOTH for both the midday and evening drawings. If no draw time is marked, the terminal will automatically print a ticket for the next available draw time. If BOTH is selected, two separate tickets will print – one for the midday drawing and one for the evening drawing.

  5. Submit your play slip

    Submit your play slip to your Florida Lottery retailer and receive your official printed PICK 3 ticket with your numbers, draw date(s), and any add-ons. You’ll need this ticket to claim any prizes, so keep it safe!

    Find a retailer

    Did you know you can download and use the Florida Lottery app to play any of our draw games?

    Our app lets you create and save your favorite numbers on digital play slips. Simply have your retailer scan the app and print your paper ticket and you’re good to go. It's that easy! Download on the App Store or Google Play.
